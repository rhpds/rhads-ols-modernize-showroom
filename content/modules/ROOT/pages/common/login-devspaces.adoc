. Switch to Red Hat OpenShift DevSpaces by clicking the **OpenShift DevSpaces** tab in the workshop interface.  

. If necessary, Log in to Dev Spaces using your OpenShift credentials:
+
NOTE: You will be initially taken to the Keycloak SSO login page
+
* Username: `{user}`
* Password: `{password}`

. After logging in, if prompted to __Authorize Access__
+
* Click **Allow selected permissions** button
+
image::common/login-devspaces.png[DevSpaces Login,link=self,window=blank,title="Logging In"]
+
CAUTION: If you see a message that says you need to authenticate again, just Click on the  **OpenShift DevSpaces** tab and it should let you into the workspace listing.

. Open the user-{user}-workspace 
+
* After logging in the workspace listing will open up and show the user-{user}-workspace entry
* Click on the **Open** link on the right side of workspace listing
+
image::common/workspace-listing.png[Open Workspace,link=self,window=blank,title="Open Workspace"]

. Wait for the workspace to initialize and open:
+
Your **kai-coolstore** gitea repository will load in DevSpaces and your development environment will be setup.
+
The workspace will open in a new tab in your browser.  The workspace tab is a VSCode GUI environment served up by DevSpaces
+
* If asked __Do you trust the authors of the files in this workspace?__, **click** __Yes, I trust the authors__
* If shown a layout introductory panel, choose **Light** theme for best contrast later in the exercise and **Mark Done**.
+
image::common/vscode-getstarted.png[VSCode Tab,link=self,window=blank,title="VSCode Tab"]


NOTE: When finished, close this browser tab and return to the Module Instructions Tab.
