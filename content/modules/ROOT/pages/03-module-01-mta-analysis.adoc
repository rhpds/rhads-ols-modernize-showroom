= MTA Assessment and Analysis: Quick-Start Guide
:toc: left
:icons: font

== 1. Environment Setup

* [cite_start]**Access the Console:** Open the *MTA Web Console*[cite: 3, 5].
* **Enable Questionnaires:**
. Switch the top-left perspective selector to *Administration*[cite: 55].
. Select *Assessment Questionnaires*[cite: 55].
. Toggle the *Required* switch for *Legacy Pathfinder* to *ON*[cite: 57, 58].
. Switch the perspective back to *Migration*[cite: 59].
* [cite_start]**Enable Insecure Repositories:** In the *Administration* perspective, go to *Repositories > Git* and toggle *Consume insecure Git repositories* to the right[cite: 102].

---

== 2. Archetype Assessment

* [cite_start]**Start Assessment:** Navigate to *Archetypes* on the left menu, click the *three-dot menu* for the *Summer Framework* archetype, and select *Assess*[cite: 65].
* **Retake & Navigate:** Click *Retake* [cite: 68], then click *Next* through the Stakeholders and initial sections (Details, Dependencies, Observability)[cite: 71, 72].
* **Update Configuration:** In the *Application cross-cutting concerns* section, find "How is the application configured?" and select: *Multiple configuration files in multiple file system locations*[cite: 74, 75].
* **Submit Review:**
. Click *Save and review*[cite: 75].
. Set *Proposed action* to *Refactor*[cite: 81, 82].
. Set *Effort estimate* to *Small*[cite: 82].
. Set *Business criticality* to *10* and *Work priority* to *9*[cite: 83].
. [cite_start]Click *Submit Review*[cite: 84].

---

== 3. Application Analysis

* **Configure Customers App:**
. [cite_start]In *Application inventory*, click the *pencil (edit) icon* for the *Customers* application[cite: 105].
. Set *Repository type* to *Git*[cite: 106].
. Enter the *Source Repository* URL, set *Branch* to `mta-lab`, and *Root path* to `customers-tomcat-legacy`[cite: 106].
. Click *Save*[cite: 107].
* **Run Analysis:**
. Select the *Customers* checkbox and click *Analyze*[cite: 114].
. **Mode:** Select *Source code*[cite: 115].
. **Targets:** Select *Containerization*, *Linux*, and *OracleJDK to OpenJDK*[cite: 118, 120].
. **Scope:** Select *Application and internal dependencies only*[cite: 124].
. **Custom Rules:** Select the *Repository* tab and enter the Gitea URL, Branch `mta-lab`, and Root path `customrules`[cite: 129, 130].
. [cite_start]**Execute:** Click *Next* through advanced options and click *Run*[cite: 133, 134].

---

== 4. Results Review

* [cite_start]**Status:** Wait for the analysis status to change from *Scheduled* to *Completed*[cite: 135, 141].
* **View Issues:** Click on the *Customers* application row to open the *Application Profile* and select the *Issues* link[cite: 143, 158].
* [cite_start]**Identify Risks:** Review the findings for *Hardcoded IP Address* and *Legacy configuration* triggers[cite: 159, 164].
